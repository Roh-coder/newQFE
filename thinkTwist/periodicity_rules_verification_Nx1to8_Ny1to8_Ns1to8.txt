Closed-form periodicity rules
===========================

Boundary conditions:
  (i + Nx, j) ~ (i, j)
  (i, j + Ny) ~ (i + Ns, j)

Directions and rules:
  p_x       = Nx
  p_y       = Nx*Ny / gcd(Nx, Ns)
  p_y_minus_x = Nx*Ny / gcd(Nx, Ny + Ns)
